Website development for Cristiano Ronaldo

Designing, programming, and developing the official website of the athlete with the most significant number of fans on social networks on the planet was, without a doubt, a challenge that we couldn’t refuse and to which we actively responded.

The website layout consisted of several menus displaying the athlete’s institutions, the latest news, achievements, and career statistics. A feature that stands out is called the “fan wall,” where any fan can interact with the website, his idol, and other Cristiano Ronaldo fans.
